Key Contributions and Impact

Designed and implemented object detection and optical character recognition (OCR) models to streamline operations for India’s largest port. The object detection model accurately identified and tracked containers, vehicles, and equipment in real-time from surveillance footage, enabling automated asset tracking and reducing manual intervention. Simultaneously, the OCR model digitized critical shipment documents such as invoices, bills of lading, and manifests, eliminating errors and delays associated with manual data entry. These integrated solutions led to a 30% reduction in operational delays, significantly improving the port’s efficiency and throughput. The project played a pivotal role in modernizing the port's logistics management system and optimizing resource allocation.
